What We Value

Hustle.

The people of Startup Exchange push to make ends meet and work to do what needs to be done. Exchangers are self-starters, persistent for what they want, and always hungry for more.

Innovation.

Startup Exchange thinks differently and challenges the status quo. We encourage people to be creative and disrupt the industries around us.

Empowerment.

Startup Exchange creates a place where people can learn and grow beyond even the Exchange by erasing the illusion of the startup world and making it accessible to anyone.

Community.

Startup Exchange fosters a community of like-minded people that supports and empowers each other to pursue their passions and live an alternative lifestyle.
