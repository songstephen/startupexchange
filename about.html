Our Values

Hustle.

The people of Startup Exchange together work, sweat, and bleed to do what needs to be done. Startup Exchangers are self-starters, persistent for what they want, and always hungry for more.

Innovation.

Startup Exchange thinks differently and challenges the status quo. We encourage people to be creative and disrupt the industries around us.

Empowerment.

Startup Exchange creates a place where people can learn and grow beyond even the Exchange by erasing the illusion of the startup world and making it accessible to anyone.

Community.

Startup Exchange fosters a community of like-minded people that support and empower each other to pursue their passions and live an alternative lifestyle.
